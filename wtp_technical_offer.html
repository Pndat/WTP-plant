Water Treatment Plant Automation System

Project: Chhuriya Water Supply Scheme – AMRUT 2.0

Prepared By: Asiatic Traders Automation Platform: Siemens S7-1500R Redundant PLC

1. System Overview

The automation system integrates the Intake Well Pump House and the 1.5 MLD Water Treatment Plant into a centralized monitoring and control system using redundant PLC architecture.

Major equipment automated:
  • Raw Water Pumps
  • Flash Mixer
  • Clarifier Bridge
  • Flocculator Drives
  • Air Blower Drives
  • Chemical Dosing Pumps
  • Clear Water Pumps
  • 33 Motorized Actuators

  • ET200MP Remote IO

6. Actuator Marshalling Philosophy

Each actuator will be wired through a marshalling panel. Signals per actuator:
  • Open Command (DO)
  • Close Command (DO)
  • Open Limit Switch (DI)
  • Close Limit Switch (DI)
  • Fault Signal (DI)
  • Remote Status (DI)
Total actuators: 33
