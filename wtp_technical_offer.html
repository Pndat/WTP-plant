Water Treatment Plant Automation System

Project: Chhuriya Water Supply Scheme – AMRUT 2.0

Prepared By: Asiatic Traders

Automation Platform: Siemens S7-1500R Redundant PLC with Intake Well Integration

1. System Overview

The automation system integrates the Intake Well Pump House and the 1.5 MLD Water Treatment Plant into a centralized monitoring and control system using redundant PLC architecture.

Major equipment automated:

  • Raw Water Pumps
  • Flash Mixer
  • Clarifier Bridge
  • Flocculator Drives
  • Air Blower Drives
  • Chemical Dosing Pumps
  • Clear Water Pumps
  • 33 Motorized Actuators with Marshalling Arrangement

Control system highlights:

  • Redundant CPU architecture using CPU-1513R
  • ET200MP remote / modular I/O architecture
  • Ethernet / fiber link between Intake Well and WTP
  • Modbus RTU over RS485 for intelligent devices
  • SCADA-ready plant visualization and diagnostics
